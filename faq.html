How can I create an account on CarSITE?

Visit our homepage and click on the "Sign Up" button. Fill in the required information and follow the instructions to create your account.

How do I list my car for sale on CarSITE?

Log in to your account, and click on the "Sell Your Car" button. Fill in all the necessary details about your car and upload pictures. Once you submit the listing, our team will review it, and it will be published on the site.

What are the fees for listing and selling my car on CarSITE?

Please visit our "Pricing and Fees" page for a detailed breakdown of our listing and transaction fees.

How can I contact the seller of a car I am interested in?

On the car listing page, click the "Contact Seller" button to send a message directly to the seller. You can ask questions, request more information, or schedule a test drive.

What should I do if I suspect a fraudulent listing or user on CarSITE?

If you suspect a fraudulent listing or user, please report it immediately by clicking the "Report" button on the listing page or contacting our support team. We take security and user safety very seriously and will investigate the issue promptly.

How long does it take for my car listing to be approved?

Our team reviews car listings within 24 hours. Once your listing has been reviewed and approved, it will appear on the website for potential buyers to view.

Can I edit my car listing after it has been submitted?

Yes, you can edit your car listing at any time by logging into your account and navigating to the "My Listings" section. Make any necessary changes and save the updates to refresh the listing on the website.

What should I include in my car listing to attract more buyers?

To attract more buyers, provide clear, high-quality photos of your car, including both exterior and interior shots. Be thorough and detailed in your description, mentioning any special features, recent maintenance, or repairs. Being honest about the car's condition and history will also help build trust with potential buyers.
